# sales-logistics-dashboard
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance and logistics efficiency within a supply chain context.

The objective is to:

Evaluate sales performance across product categories and marketplaces

Monitor logistics operations and delivery performance

Identify operational bottlenecks and delays

Support data-driven decision-making in sales and supply chain management

## The dashboard is structured into two main analytical pages:

Sales Performance Analysis

Logistics & Delivery Analysis


## 1- Sales Performance Dashboard

This page provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across different dimensions.

#### Key analyses include:

Revenue and sales trends over time

Performance by product category

Performance across different marketplaces

Contribution analysis by customer segments

Identification of top-performing and underperforming categories

#### Insights generated:

Clear visibility of revenue concentration across product lines

Identification of high-margin and low-performing categories

Marketplaces with the strongest and weakest performance

Strategic opportunities for portfolio optimization and targeted marketing actions


## 2- Logistics & Delivery Dashboard

This page focuses on operational efficiency and supply chain performance.

#### Key metrics analyzed:

Delivery time performance

Delay frequency and distribution

Regional performance comparison

Bottleneck identification

#### Main findings:

Identification of logistics bottlenecks affecting customer satisfaction

Performance gaps between regions and product flows

Operational inefficiencies impacting overall service level

The dashboard supports performance monitoring and enables decision-makers to prioritize corrective actions.


## Tools & Techniques

Power BI Desktop

Data modeling (relationships, calculated columns, measures)

DAX for KPI calculations

Interactive filters and slicers

Dashboard design for executive reporting
